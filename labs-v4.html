FAQ

Common Questions

What is a gut health panel and who needs it?

Our gut health panel includes stool analysis, microbiome markers, H. pylori testing, and digestive enzyme assessment. Recommended for people with bloating, IBS, food intolerances, or those on a wellness journey.

Do you offer home sample collection in Pune?

Yes. We offer home collection across Pune including Baner, Aundh, Wakad, Kothrud, and Hinjewadi. Book online and our trained phlebotomist arrives at your preferred time.

How long does it take to get wellness test results?

Standard blood panels are ready in 4–6 hours. Specialised wellness panels (hormone, gut, genomic) take 24–48 hours. All reports include doctor-reviewed interpretation notes.

What is included in the Wellness Pro package?

The Wellness Pro package (₹2,499, 55 parameters) includes all Starter tests plus gut health markers, full hormone panel, and metabolic screening — a comprehensive picture of your internal health.

Is Lifeline Wellness Labs NABL accredited?

Yes. Lifeline Wellness Labs is NABL accredited, ensuring test results meet national quality standards. We follow strict sample handling and quality control protocols for accurate, reliable reports.
