Patient Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What does an MRI scan cost? +

MRI scans from ₹4,500 (single region -- brain, spine, knee), contrast MRI from ₹8,000. Full-body MRI from ₹15,000. Discounts on pre-booking and weekend appointments.

Do you offer home sample collection in Mumbai? +

Yes -- across Malad, Andheri, Borivali, Goregaon, and Kandivali. Book before 8 AM for same-morning collection. Reports sent digitally within 24 hours.

How long does a CT scan take? +

10-30 minutes including preparation. Contrast CT requires 30 min wait post-injection. Reports ready in 2-4 hours and shared digitally.

Are you open on Sundays? +

Yes -- Sundays 8 AM to 2 PM for urgent tests and blood collection. Imaging (MRI, CT) available Mon-Sat.

What preventive health packages do you offer? +

Basic ₹1,499 (40 tests), Comprehensive ₹2,999 (75 tests incl. cardiac + hormonal), Executive ₹4,999 (100+ tests, vitamins, cancer markers, ECG). Annual packages include free repeat tests.
