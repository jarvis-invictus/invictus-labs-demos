Frequently Asked Questions

What blood tests do you offer? +

All routine tests: CBC, LFT, KFT, blood sugar (fasting, PP, HbA1c), lipid profile, thyroid (TSH, T3, T4), vitamin D, B12, iron studies. Plus specialised infection, hormone, and fertility tests.

Do you have a senior citizen health package? +

Yes -- ₹1,499 (55+ tests: cardiac, diabetes, kidney, liver, thyroid, bone health). Reports delivered home. 10% discount for patients over 60.

Do you offer pregnancy-related tests? +

Yes -- beta-hCG, anomaly scan package, TORCH panel, gestational diabetes (GCT/GTT), and post-natal thyroid screening.

What are your timings? Open Sundays? +

Mon-Sat 7 AM-8 PM | Sunday 7 AM-12 PM. Early morning slots for fasting tests.

Can I book tests online? +

Yes -- online, WhatsApp, or call. Book home collection before 9 AM for same-day service. Confirmation + report download link sent immediately.
