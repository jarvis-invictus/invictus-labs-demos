FAQ

Common Questions

What is precision medicine diagnostics?

Precision medicine diagnostics goes beyond standard blood tests to analyse genetic markers, hormonal balance, metabolic pathways, and microbiome -- giving you personalised insights rather than population-average reference ranges.

How is NovaDx different from a regular diagnostic lab?

NovaDx uses advanced testing technologies (liquid biopsy, genomic sequencing, microbiome analysis) not available at standard labs. Every report includes detailed interpretation and an optional specialist consultation.

Do you offer home sample collection in Pune?

Yes. We provide home collection across Pune including Magarpatta, Kalyani Nagar, Koregaon Park, Hadapsar, and Kharadi. Our team follows strict cold-chain protocols for precision samples.

How long do NovaDx test results take?

Standard panels (Essential ₹1,299) are ready in 4-8 hours. Precision Pro panels take 24-48 hours. Genomic Health reports are delivered in 7-10 business days with a specialist consultation included.

Is NovaDx NABL accredited?

Yes. NovaDx Diagnostics is NABL accredited, following ISO 15189 standards for medical testing. This ensures accuracy and reliability across all panels including advanced genomic and microbiome testing.
